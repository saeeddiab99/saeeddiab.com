Saeed Diab – Pianist

Concert Pianist based in Berlin

Born in 1999 to a Palestinian family in the Galilee, Saeed Diab is a concert pianist based in Berlin. He studied at the Buchmann-Mehta School of Music in Tel Aviv and later earned his Artist Diploma from the Barenboim-Said Akademie, where he worked closely with Sir András Schiff.

Saeed has won top prizes in national competitions and has performed widely as a soloist, chamber musician, and with orchestras across Europe and the Middle East. His appearances include international festivals and prestigious venues.

He has participated in masterclasses with renowned artists including Daniel Barenboim, Oxana Yablonskaya, Gilbert Kalish, and Norma Fisher.
